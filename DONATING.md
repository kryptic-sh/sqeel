# Donate

If sqeel saves you time, consider supporting development.

sqeel is and always will be free and open source. There is no Pro tier, no
paywall, no telemetry, no ads. Donations fund the project.

## Donor priority

Donors get priority on bug reports and feature requests. Open an issue, link
your donation (Ko-fi handle, tx hash, whatever), and it jumps the queue. That's
the whole deal — no tiers, no SLA, no contracts.
